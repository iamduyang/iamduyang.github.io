Brief Biography

I graduated from Big Data Intelligence Lab, Institute of Software Chinese Academy of Sciences(ISCAS), researching on data mining and machine learning difficulties, focusing on developing effective, efficient, easy to use techniques for academic and industrial use. Prior to joining Big Data Intelligence Lab, I received my B.Eng. degree in 2015 in Electronic Information Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology(HUST).

Currently, my research interests include social network mining and natural language processing.
